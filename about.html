The Beginning

A boutique firm, built on a slightly contrarian idea.

Cohen & Associates was founded in 2004 on a simple premise: that serious financial expertise and genuinely personal service don't have to be mutually exclusive. Most firms ask you to pick one. We were built to deliver both.

Today we're a team of more than fifteen accountants and specialists serving the full spectrum — start-ups finding their footing, established corporations with real complexity, and high-net-worth individuals whose finances deserve careful, discreet attention.

We represent businesses and individuals before the IRS and state authorities in all fifty states, and we handle every part of the picture: planning, preparation, compliance, and the kind of year-round advisory that means you're never caught off guard.

We treat every return as if it were our own.
