Our Story

Building Trust Through Excellence

Are you tired of the same old accounting firms that treat you like just another number? Look no further than Cohen & Associates, a local accounting firm founded by siblings, Oksana and Steve Inoyatov. Their inspiring story of hard work, dedication, and expertise in the field of accounting is one that deserves recognition. Immigrating from Bukhara, Uzbekistan in 2001, Oksana and Steve had a dream to build something extraordinary. Oksana's pursuit of excellence led her to pursue a degree in accounting and economics at Queens college. Oksana graduated at the top of her class with honors and gained invaluable experience at a top 4 accounting firm, Deloitte. Throughout her career, she has worked with renowned brands such as Pepsi, Coca Cola, Deloitte and Touch√©, RSM McGladrey. Her expertise extends to closely-held small and midsize businesses, as well as servicing high-net-worth individuals and celebrities. Following in his sister's footsteps, Steve joined university to complete a double major in accounting and information systems, as well as an economics degree with cum laude in accounting. With his expertise in financial auditing, Oksana offers him to join Protax Services very well known boutique firm that offers international taxation to a high net worth individuals. Working for a top 4 accounting firm, PwC, provided invaluable experience, but his true passion was working closely with high-net-worth individuals and closely-held businesses. He even played a pivotal role in building full financial controls at the largest event venue in New York City. With a team consisting of certified public accountants, enrolled agents, certified financial planners, and various attorneys, Cohen & Associates offers a wide range of services tailored to different industries and high-net-worth individuals. They have a particular affinity for providing exceptional service to dental, medical, law, restaurants, accounting practices, solo practitioners, and real estate professionals. What sets Cohen & Associates apart from the rest is their personalized approach. They understand that each client's situation is unique and deserves individual attention. Services offered include tax structures, tax saving strategies, wealth and asset accumulation and retention, business tax compliance, bookkeeping services, payroll services, and much more. In a world of regular accountants, Cohen & Associates prides themselves on being "Not just your regular accountants." Their dedication to exceptional service, deep expertise, and a genuine care for their clients' financial well-being set them apart from the competition. Whether you're an individual, a small business owner, or a high-net-worth individual, Cohen & Associates is here to help.
