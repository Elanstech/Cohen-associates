Service 01

Tax Strategy & Compliance

Tax shouldn't be a once-a-year scramble. We build a year-round strategy that legally minimizes what you owe and keeps you airtight with the IRS and every state and country you touch.

What's included

  • Proactive Tax Planning

    Multi-year projections and timing of income, deductions, and distributions to shrink your effective rate.

  • IRS & State Representation

    Enrolled Agents stand in for you on audits, notices, liens, and appeals — federal and state.

  • Entity Structuring

    S-corp, C-corp, LLC, and partnership selection or restructuring to optimize how you're taxed.

  • Credits & Incentives

    R&D credits, QBI, energy incentives, and industry-specific deductions you may be leaving behind.

  • International & FATCA

    Foreign income, FBAR, treaty positions, and transfer pricing handled cleanly and compliantly.

  • Return Preparation

    Federal, state, and local filings prepared, reviewed, and filed accurately — and on time.

What you walk away with

  • A lower effective tax rate
  • No surprise notices or penalties
  • Audit-ready documentation, always
